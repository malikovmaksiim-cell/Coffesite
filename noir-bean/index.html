Our Story

Crafted with Passion. Served with Elegance.

Noir Bean was born from a singular obsession: to elevate the daily ritual of coffee into an extraordinary experience. We source only the top 1% of specialty grade Arabica beans from sustainable microlots across the globe.

Our masterful roasters and award-winning baristas treat every extraction as an art form, balancing exact science with creative intuition to deliver perfection in every cup.

Our Process

From Farm to Cup.

We believe that exceptional coffee starts at the source. By partnering directly with small-holder farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, we ensure transparent, ethical trade that benefits the growers.

Our roasting process is a delicate balance of art and science. Using small-batch drum roasters, we meticulously profile every lot to highlight its unique terroir—bringing out vibrant fruit notes, deep chocolates, and delicate florality.
